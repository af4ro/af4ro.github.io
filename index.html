Intro

If I had to describe Anshul in two words,
I'd definitely call him an " Inquisitive Traveller ".
Sounds absurd until you see his passion for traveling the world.
Sounds exaggerated till you see his eyes filled with "The hope of learning something new every single day".

On the rare occasions when you don't see Anshul stuck to some tech blogs on the internet; you can find him appreciating unusual patterns in the day to day life. Intrigued by simplicity and minimalism -
This lad makes a marvelous Photographer.

Anshul started off in the Suburbs of Pune (India) and still calls it home. However, he is currently in the United States of America studying Computer Science at the prestigious University of California, Irvine.


In the words of Anshul Singhal.

"I'm a visual thinker, really... I like to wander off into wild fields in search of something 'Alag', something extraordinary; all I carry alongside is my compass of curiosity. This was one of the reasons I picked Photography as a hobby and got motivated to walk foreign lands. I've met thousands of interesting people on this long journey and have heard their stories. Meeting new people and getting to know their cultures is something that brings stability to my extrovert-ish personality.

In the words of Frank Borman:-
"Exploration is really the essence of the human spirit"

When I'm not outside soaking sunlight, I'm working on my Startup and on some other side projects. I chose to divert my potential towards Computer Science in the hope of being exposed to cutting-edge technology at all points of time and being able to 'hack' them into something that benefits everyone. Being able to interpret and invent technology is one of the best feelings ever, and this is what I live for."

As Steve Jobs once said:
"People who are crazy enough to think they can change the world are the ones who do"
